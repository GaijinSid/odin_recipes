Sweet Potato Stacks

An easy and tasty side dish!

These stacks are a great accompaniment to many dishes and require minimal effort

Ingredients

Required
  • Two Sweet Potatoes, thinly sliced
  • Olive Oil
  • Parmesan Cheese, grated

Steps

  1. Coat the thin slices of sweet potato in olive oil and mix with parmesan cheese
  2. Stack slices 5 or 6 high and top with extra parmesan. (A cupcake tray works great for this)
  3. Bake @200c for 30 minutes
