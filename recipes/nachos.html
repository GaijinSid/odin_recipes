BBQ Pulled Chicken Nachos

An easily customisable, family friendly treat!

This nachos recipe is a great option for picky eaters and the time constrained.

Substitue the chicken for your favourite alternatives and finish your bowl with your favourite toppings.

Ingredients

Required
  • Nacho Chips
  • Grated Cheese
  • Salsa
  • 2 Chicken Breasts
  • BBQ Seasoning
Optional
  • Sour Cream
  • Jalapenos
  • Salad Toppings (Lettuce, Tomato, Cucumber)

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 200 degrees
  2. Add BBQ seasoning to chicken breast and cook in oven for 30-40 minutes
  3. Arrange nacho chips in bowls and top with salsa and cheese
  4. Pull apart cooked chicken breasts and layer onto nachos
  5. Add cheese and microwave bowls for 2 minutes
  6. Add any additional toppings to preference
